About Event

This October, the first edition of a student-held hackathon, Version β will be hosted by Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology. Our renowned institute is known for its diversity, scenic beauty and is, among the top ranked colleges in India, but unfortunately, the idea of Hackathon still remains a foreign concept. We aim to provide a platform for our participants where, they can nurture their true potential, inculcate innovative development and get an opportunity to interact with bright minds of the country.

Version β, is the brainchild of few unorthodox driven students of Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology who wanted to move away from the realm of normal academic routine. Organizing a Hackathon, opens up new dimensions for the participants to create something extraordinary within a limited timespan. Maybe even, just 36 hours!

Our prime focus is to, blur the boundaries of imagination. Age is an issue of mind over matter. And hence, our event is open to one and all. We'll take care of the food and stay - virtually leaving you no excuse to build something that'll blow our minds.

FAQs

What is Version β?

Version β is the annual Hackathon organized at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology, Bhopal. It is one of the largest student-organized hackathons in Central India.


Registration - as a team or individually ?

You can do either of them. If you do not have a team member, you will be assigned as a team with other individual participants on the event commencement day itself.


Who runs the Version β?

Version β is run by ISTE SC MANIT(Indian Society for Technical Education Students' Chapter MANIT, BHOPAL).


Who can Participate?

Anyone having a valid student ID card of their college or university is eligible to take part in the competition.


What should I bring?

We’ll send you a comprehensive list once it’s closer to showtime, but it’ll be basically what you’d need for any sleepover: laptop, charger, deodorant (!), change of clothes. You won’t need food or drinks; we’ve got you covered there.


Can a team have members from different colleges?

Yes.


How would I know whether I am selected or not?

We will revert back to you with e-mails provided on the registration form.


Will I be provided with accomodation?

Yes, participants selected for the event will be hosted in the Manit Bhopal campus. Food and accomodation for the entire duration of the event will be provided for the team members only.


When will the registration open?

The registration will commence from 20 September, 2018 and will be open till 20 October, 2018.


How many persons can be there in one team?

One team can have a minimum of 1 and maximum of 4 members.


Having any other query

The ISTE team is eagerly waiting for you on Facebook or mail us instead.
