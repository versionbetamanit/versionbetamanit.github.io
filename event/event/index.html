About Event

Version β, a student-held hackathon in India will host it's first edition this October. The regions we come from are known for their diversity, scenic beauty and some of the best colleges in India but sadly, not for hackathons. That's where we come in. We aim to inculcate innovative development and provide a medium through which bright minds from everywhere can channel out their true potential.

Version β started out as a dorm room idea this year by a few unorthodox driven students of MANIT BHOPAL who wanted to move away from the realm of the normal academic routine and organise a hackathon to help expand the thinking of students and show that you don't always need a lifetime to make something extraordinary. Sometimes, you can do it in 36 hours too.

Our focus is on letting your imagination flow free and enable you to push the boundaries of what can be done. Age is an issue of mind over matter. We don't mind, so it doesn't matter. Hence, we're open for all! We'll take care of the food, travel and stay - virtually leaving you no excuse to not build something that'll blow our minds.

FAQs

What is Version β?

Version β is the annual Hackathon organized at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology, Bhopal. It is one of the largest student-organized hackathons in Central India.


What if I’m the only one selected in my team?

Acceptances are rolled out individually. If 50% of the team is individually selected, the entire of the team gets selected. If not, please contact us immediately so that we can review your team again.


Who runs the Version β?

Version β is run by ISTE (Technical Society of MANIT BHOPAL)


Can we apply as a team?

Not quite — you and your intended teammates should all submit individual applications. But if you get an invitation and some of your teammates don’t, shoot us an email at iste@manit.ac.in and we’ll try to sort things out


What should I bring?

We’ll send you a comprehensive list once it’s closer to showtime, but it’ll be basically what you’d need for any sleepover: laptop, charger, deodorant (!), change of clothes. You won’t need food or drinks; we’ve got you covered there.


How and when will I hear back?

We’ll be releasing decisions weekly — if you’ve submitted your application by a Friday, you’ll be getting an email back by the next Friday!


Who can apply?

If you’re enrolled in a high school, college, or university, or if you left school during the current school year, you’re good to go!


What do people normally make?

You can make anything you like. Most of the hacks revolve around web or phone apps, or working with hardware.


What are you looking for in an application?

Above anything else — passion. Tell us what you care about, what excites you. We want to know what’ll drive you to start and continue hacking, whether it’s your first time or your fifteenth. If there’s something about you that stands out from the “typical” hacker, we’d love to hear about that, too!


How much does it cost to attend?

Zero. Version β is free for everyone.


Will you be offering travel reimbursements?

We would support you if you can't afford to travel to Version β. Shoot us a mail if you need reimbursements.


When are the applications closing?

Applications close on ---! If you haven’t submitted by then, we’ll review whatever’s there.
